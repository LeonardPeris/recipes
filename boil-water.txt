Ingredients:
	1. 500 mL tap water

Utensils:
	1. Heavy bottom saucepan

Steps:
	1. Transfer water into saucepan
	2. Place saucepan on burner
	3. Turn on fire at max setting
	4. Water will take approximately 15 minutes to boil
	5. Once water surface starts shaking vigorously and bubbles 
		can be seen rising from the bottom, the process in complete
	6. Turn off gas and remove saucepan from burner. Use protective 
		gear as the saucepan will be hot.
	7. Let water cool down to room temperature before consuming.
